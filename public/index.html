Wynn Mustin's Playlist "Taking McKibben Street": Songs from the era in which I began grad school, an affair, and subsequently moved from the cushy apartment I shared with my then boyfriend to a Craigslist flophouse in the Mckibbin lofts. Mckibbin street represents a relatively traumatic time of my life and these songs both try to undo and embrace that.

Arguably my favorite song of the era. It reminds me of the adrenaline/serotonin/anxiety of straying from the "good on paper" boyfriend I had so palpably outgrown.
